About

Hi! I'm Grace Hatamyar, a Software Developer and recent Code Fellows alum currently based in Seattle, WA. On my blog, I write about the things I'm learning, the projects I'm building, and anything I don't want to forget. It was built using Pelican, and is hosted on GitHub Pages. The theme is a fork of Giulio Fidente's svb-hack. You can find my changes here.

My background is in Urban Studies, so my interests tend to lie at the interesection of Urban Planning and Technology. I especially like building apps that have anything to do with mapping or open data. I also lived in Buenos Aires for two years after college, working as a Spanish-to-English Translator and Editor. If you'd like to know more, you can download my resume here.
